Product Management ki Duniya

Is section mein hum Product Management ke fundamentals ko samjhenge. Ek Product Manager (PM) kaun hota hai, uski zaroori skills kya hain, aur kaise har product apni life mein alag-alag stages se guzarta hai.

Product Manager Kaun Hai? (The "Mini-CEO")

Ek Product Manager product ki success ka maalik hota hai. Woh ek pul (bridge) ki tarah kaam karta hai jo technology team (Engineers), design team, aur business team (Sales, Marketing) ko jodta hai. Uska kaam hai yeh decide karna ki customer ke liye **kya banana hai**, **kyun banana hai**, aur team ke saath milkar use **kaise banana hai**.

Product Lifecycle: Har Product ki Kahani

Har product in 4 stages se guzarta hai. Niche diye gaye chart mein stages par click karke unke baare mein jaanein.

Introduction

Product market mein naya-naya aata hai. Focus awareness badhane par hota hai.

Tech ki Buniyaad

Ek Technical PM ke liye technology ki buniyaad samajhna bahut zaroori hai. Yahan hum Web/Mobile Architecture, Databases aur System Design ke core concepts ko explore karenge.

Frontend vs. Backend

Har app ke do hisse hote hain:

  • Frontend: Woh hissa jo aapko dikhta hai (Buttons, Forms). Yeh aapke browser ya phone mein chalta hai.
  • Backend: Parde ke peeche ka kaam (Server, Database). Yahan saara data process aur store hota hai.

APIs: The Waiter

API (Application Programming Interface) ek waiter ki tarah hai jo Frontend (customer) aur Backend (kitchen) ke beech communication karwata hai. Yeh do alag software ko aapas mein baat karne ka zariya hai.

Database: SQL vs. NoSQL

SQL (Relational)

Data structured tables (jaise Excel) mein store hota hai. Example: User data table.

NoSQL (Non-Relational)

Data flexible documents mein store hota hai. Big Data aur real-time apps ke liye behtar. Example: Social media posts.

System Design: Jab App Bada Ho Jaye

Scalability

Traffic badhne par system ki capacity ko aasani se badhana.

Load Balancing

Incoming traffic ko alag-alag servers par baantna.

Caching

Baar-baar use hone wale data ko fast memory mein store karna.

Product Banane ka Process

Idea se lekar launch tak, product banane ka ek poora process hota hai. Is section mein hum Agile development, Product Strategy, User Experience (UX) design aur Data Analytics ke baare mein jaanenge.

Agile vs. Waterfall

Yeh software banane ke do alag-alag tareeke hain.

Waterfall (Purana Tareeka) üêå

Saari planning ek saath, fir design, fir coding. Launch ke baad hi feedback milta hai. Badlaav karna mushkil.

Agile (Naya Tareeka) üöÄ

Kaam ko chhote hisson (sprints) mein baantna. Har sprint ke baad feedback lena aur sudhaar karna. Badlaav karna aasaan.

Product Strategy & Roadmap

Strategy (Lambi Soch): Agle 2-3 saal mein product ko kahan le jaana hai.

Roadmap (Raasta): Strategy tak pahunchne ka high-level plan ki agle 3-12 mahino mein kya banayenge.

Data Analytics & A/B Testing

Analytics: User data ko study karke product ko behtar banana. Jaise, kitne log app use kar rahe hain (DAU/MAU).

A/B Testing: Ek hi feature ke 2 version alag-alag users ko dikhakar test karna ki kaunsa behtar hai.

Career Guide

Product Management ek exciting career hai. Yahan hum PM ki naukri paane, interview ki taiyari karne aur career mein aage badhne ke tareekon par baat karenge.

The PM Interview: Kya Pucha Jaata Hai?

PM interviews aapki problem-solving skills ko test karte hain. Ek naya sawaal dekhne ke liye button par click karein.

Product Sense/Design

Career Progression Path

Associate Product Manager

Ek senior PM ke under kaam seekhna.

Product Manager

Ek feature ya chhote product ko own karna.

Senior Product Manager

Ek bade aur complex product ko manage karna.

Director/VP of Product

Multiple product lines aur PMs ko manage karna.
